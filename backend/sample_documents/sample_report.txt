2026 CUSTOMER SUPPORT OPERATIONS REPORT

Executive Summary
Average first-response time improved from 9.2 hours to 5.6 hours during the first quarter.
Customer satisfaction rose to 92 percent. The largest remaining issue is weekend coverage.

Findings
The billing queue represented 31 percent of all tickets.
Self-service articles resolved approximately 18 percent of incoming questions.
Weekend response times averaged 14.4 hours.

Recommendations
Add a rotating weekend support shift.
Expand billing documentation and surface it inside the product.
Review response-time and satisfaction metrics monthly.
