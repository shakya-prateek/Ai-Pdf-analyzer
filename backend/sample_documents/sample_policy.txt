REMOTE WORK AND INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY
Effective date: January 1, 2026

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.
Company devices must use full-disk encryption and automatic security updates.
Confidential documents may not be stored on personal devices or personal cloud accounts.
Employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems from public networks.
Suspected loss of a device or disclosure of confidential information must be reported to Security within one hour.
Repeated violations may result in removal of remote-work privileges.
